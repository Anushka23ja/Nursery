Micro Irrigation

Micro-Irrigation System is the most efficient method of irrigation. Hence for successful installation, we provide various technical services such as land survey, water & soil.

• Foggers and Micro Sprinklers:

> Suitable for nurseries, green /poly houses, poultry houses, home gardens.
> Increase humidity and lowers temperature.
> Easy assembly and disassembly.
> Available in 1 to 1.5 meter radius.

• Drip Irrigation System:

> Saves water, labour and time for irrigation.
> Increase in production > Efficient management.
> Suitable for fruit crops/vegetables/floriculture.
> Improves quality of produce.

• Sprinkler Irrigation System:

> Uniform application of water.
> Suitable for shallow rooted crops
> Saves water, labour & time.
> Easy to install and transferable.
> Highly resistant to mechanical damages.

• Pop-Up System:

> Uniform and even distribution of water.
> Enhances the aesthetic value of landscape.
> Saves water, labour and time.
> Available in various ranges from 1.5 to 1.9 meter radius with or without automation.
> Suitable for small gardens, residential complex, factories, hotels, resorts, lawn and playgrounds.

• Irriguns System:

> Suitable for large gardens, sports grounds, public parks, golf course, turfs.
>Highly economical systems.
>Light, but strong die cast aluminum body.
>Highly energy efficient and covers large area.
>Available in various ranges from 90 to 120 feet radius with angular adjustment.
