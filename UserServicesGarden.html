Garden Execution And Maintenance.

The design of a garden combines the static, fixed architectural features and the dynamic, living structure of the garden: the trees, shrubs, hedges, herbaceous plants, grass and others. It takes in accountf harmony of farm, texture and color. We undertake garden designing, execution and maintenance.

We have successfully undertaken plantscape execution and maintainance projects at a number of sites which include residential bungalows, restaurants, terrace gardens and corporate gardens.


Our team consists of expert horticulturists and trained and skilled gardeners having sound knowledge of plants. This staff ensures quality execution and maintenance of all undertaken projects.



Under the Garden Maintenance Contract we offer the following services:

• Briefing

The caretaker will be briefed about the processes that have to be carried out in between every visit.

• Training

Parts of the briefing which require practical application will be demonstrated to the caretaker till such time that the person is capable of managing independently.

• Assessments

The representative will periodically inspect the site and assess the soil, water, climatic conditions and topography of the plot to identify any changes that may affect the health and growth of the plants.

• Recape

The briefings, training and assessments will be recapped on every successive visit to ensure that suitable action has been taken in all possible perspectives.

• Treatment

Plants which are damaged and within the limits of being recovered, so as to achieve its original growth line and expected growth results, will be restored. Similarly, plants will be periodically treated with organic disinfectants to prevent them from being infested with insects/ pests, etc.

• Manure & Fertilizer Applications

Natural manure and other nutritional elements will be supplemented. In case of special supplements to specific species of plants, the same will be prescribed. The caretaker will be instructed regarding the frequency of the supplements prescribed.

• Transplantation

This refers to plants that require to be shifted to new areas within the plot, either because of topographical requirements or for the purpose of creating or utilization of space.

• Pruning & Mowing

This includes trimming of plants and lawns to beautify the garden.

• Topography Alterations

This refers to any sought of alignment or ploughing of the land, which could benefit growth or health of the plants.
