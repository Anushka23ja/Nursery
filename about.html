About Us

Live Your Life With Plants...!



A Nursery is a place where plants are grown,nurtured and sold out.Nurseries supply plants for gardens, for agriculture, for forestry and for conservation biology.

Nurseries can grow plants in open fields, on container fields and in tunnels or greenhouses.



Welcome to Plant Nursery



Our Plant Nursery is producing, cultivating and marketing different kinds of plants all over the country and we also undertake projects of landscaping. We own thousands of varieties of plants in the plant kingdom.

With able assistance we are managing the group of Landscape Architects, Horticulturist and other staff.
