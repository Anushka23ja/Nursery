Career In Plant Nursery

1. Position : Customer Care Executive

Qualification/Education : Graduation in any Stream

Experience : Minimum 2 Years Experience

Job Location : Kolhapur

Job Profile: - Receive inbound calls and make outbound call with reference to the business.

- Preparation of Landscape Proposals/Offers as Per the Needs of the Client.

- Assist in the fulfillment of orders and resolve potential order issues.

- Preparation of Marketing Manuals and Catalogs.

- Fluency in Hindi Must. Language of English and Marathi will be an Advantage.



2. Position : Horticulturist/Landscape Manager

Qualification/Education : Graduation in any Stream

Experience : Minimum 2 Years Experience

Job Location : Kolhapur

Job Profile:- Must Have Good Knowledge of Plants & Site Execution ( Plantatation Work ).

- Must have Knowledge of Basic Computer Skills & Microsoft Office

- Must be Able to Communicate with Clients, Horticulturists and Engineers.

- Travelling Flexibilty Welcome.

- Must be Fluent in Communication in Hindi and Marathi. Fluency in English will be an Additional Advantage.
