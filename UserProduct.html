Amazon Lily(Victoria amazonica).


Truly a plant to wow your friends and neighbors. In a large pond, this plant's leaves can reach 6 feet across and are covered in spiny prickles. The flowers, which appear in summer, start as thorn-covered green buds that open to large white masterpieces that fade pink. The blooms have the fragrance of ripe pineapple.

LIGHT: Sun

TYPE: Water

HEIGHT: Under 6 inches

WIDTH: To 20 feet wide

FLOWER COLOR: White

SEASONAL FEATURES: Summer Bloom

SPECIAL FEATURES:Fragrance,Good for Containers, Low Maintenance
